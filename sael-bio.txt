Sael Lee is  currently a Professor in the Dept. of Software and Computer Engineering and Dept. of Artificial Intelligence at Ajou University, Republic of Korea. 
From 2012.9-2018.2 she was an Assistant Professor in Dept. of Computer Science at SUNY (Stony Brook University) Korea.  She received her Ph.D. in Computer Science from Purdue University in 2010, and her B.S. in Computer Science from Korea University in 2005.  She has published in major journals and proceedings using her publication name, "Lee Sael", in the areas of interpretable machine learning, tensor analysis/data mining, and bioinformatics.
